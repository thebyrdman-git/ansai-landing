### You Need 3 Accounts:

- Long-term wealth building

## ğŸš¨ Common Questions

### "What if I don't make enough to save 10%?"
Start with **1%**. Even $50/month builds the habit. Increase as income grows.

### "What if my bills vary (like utilities)?"
Add a 15-20% buffer to your Bill Pay Checking allocation. Excess rolls over.

### "What if I run out in Primary Checking?"
That's the point! It forces awareness. Adjust your split next month.

### "Do I need Ansai for this?"
**No!** This is basic automation using your bank's built-in features. It's free and works great.
